About

The Book of Mormon: Parallel Reader is a project by me (Jake Searle), a computer science undergrad from Brigham Young University. Check out my other projects here:

Inspiration

I served a full-time mission in Berlin Germany, and as I was there, I met members and friends of the Church that spoke all sorts of languages. I myself tried learning Chinese, in addition to German to help these people learn the Gospel, and saw what a difference it made for people to understand something in their native tongue.

While studying Chinese, I fell in love with the Chinese language study edition of the Book of Mormon and also thebible.org's parallel Bible reader.
